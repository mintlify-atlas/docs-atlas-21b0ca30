After completing the purity test, you're redirected to the results page at `/results?score=X` where X is your calculated purity score.

## Understanding Your Score

The score represents your "purity" percentage based on how many SJSU experiences you've had:

- **0 = Done everything** — You've checked all 100 items and have experienced everything the test covers
- **100 = Campus virgin** — You haven't checked any items (maximum purity)

The lower your score, the more SJSU experiences you've had. The higher your score, the fewer campus experiences you've encountered.

## Score Interpretation

Here's a fun breakdown of what different score ranges might mean:

| Score Range | Title | Description |
| ----------- | ----- | ----------- |
| 80-100 | Fresh Spartan | New to campus life, lots of experiences ahead |
| 50-79 | Well-rounded | You've had your share of SJSU moments |
| 20-49 | Seasoned veteran | You've seen and done quite a bit around campus |
| 0-19 | Legend | You've truly experienced SJSU to the fullest |

<Note>
These interpretations are just for fun — there's no right or wrong score!
</Note>

## Sharing Your Results

The results page includes a "Share Result" button that copies a pre-formatted message to your clipboard:

```
I got ${score} on the SJSU Purity Test! Try it yourself: ${origin}
```

After clicking the button:
1. The text is copied to your clipboard
2. The button temporarily shows "Copied!" for 2 seconds
3. Paste and share wherever you'd like!

## Navigation Options

From the results page, you can:

- **Retake the test** — Click the link back to `/` to try again
- **View statistics** — Click the link to `/stats` to see how your score compares to others
- **Follow updates** — Visit [x.com/danielung19](https://x.com/danielung19) for project updates
