# The Purity Test

The SJSU Purity Test is a 100-question quiz that measures your campus life experiences at San José State University. Each question describes a specific scenario or experience related to SJSU, and you check off the items you've done.

## How It Works

The test presents 100 checkbox questions covering various aspects of SJSU campus life. Simply check the boxes for experiences you've had, then submit to receive your purity score.

The scoring formula calculates your percentage of "purity":

```javascript
const score = Math.round(((questions.length - checkedCount) / questions.length) * 100)
```

**How scoring works:**
- More checked items = lower score (less pure)
- Fewer checked items = higher score (more pure)
- A score of 100 means you checked nothing (maximum purity)
- A score of 0 means you checked everything (minimum purity)

<Note>
The purity test is meant for fun and nostalgia. It's not a bucket list — we don't recommend trying to check off every item!
</Note>

## Taking the Test

To take the purity test:

1. Navigate to the home page at `/`
2. Read through all 100 questions
3. Check the boxes for experiences you've had
4. Click submit to see your score
5. Your answers and score are saved to the database

After submission, you'll be redirected to the results page where you can view your score and share it with friends.
